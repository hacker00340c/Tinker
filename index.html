Tinkerfest 2020-21

DAV PUBLIC SCHOOL, SECTOR 49, UPPAL SOUTHEND, GURUGRAM, HARYANA

In these difficult times of COVID-19 where everyone is bounded at their homes, DAV Public School, Sec 49, Gurugram aims to inculcate future skills and scientific temperament in the young learners of today through an online Interschool Competition – TINKERFEST 2020-21, under Atal Tinkering Lab, an initiative by NITI AAYOG and Atal Innovation Mission.

This contest aims to recognize and nurture young talent to think of ingenious ideas to solve global issues. During this global pandemic, the youth have stepped up and pitched in with creative and innovative ideas to bring joy, solve challenges, help those in need and provide balance to the misbalanced ecosystem.

TINKERFEST is scheduled from 11th January 2021 to 6th February 2021 and students from Grades 2 to 11 can avail the oppurtunity to be a part of these unique and marvelous competitions that will enhance their creative and critical thinking abilities.
